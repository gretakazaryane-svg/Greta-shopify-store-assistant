About Me

Why retail experience matters for your eCommerce store

Years of working directly with customers in retail and banking taught me one thing that most virtual assistants don't have — I genuinely understand what customers need and how to keep them satisfied.

I've worked as a shop assistant at ELF Cosmetics, Bershka, and Fresh Line, and progressed to Customer Service Hall Supervisor at Evocabank — managing customer experience, coordinating operations, and making fast decisions under pressure. That retail and customer-facing background translates directly into how I manage Shopify stores.

I help store owners with product listing, catalog organization, store admin, order tracking, and customer communication. I understand how products should be presented to attract buyers, and I bring the same attention to detail to every task I take on.

I'm organized, reliable, and easy to work with. If you need a Shopify assistant who understands both the customer side and the operational side of eCommerce — let's talk.
